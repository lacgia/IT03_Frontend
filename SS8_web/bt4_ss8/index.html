Nia Honjo

Nia Honjo (本ほん条じょう 二に亜あ, Honjō Nia?) is the ninth Spirit to appear in the series.

Origami Tobiichi

Origami Tobiichi (鳶とび一いち 折おり紙がみ, Tobiichi Origami?) is one of the main female characters of the Date A Live series. She is Shido Itsuka's classmate, who is famous for her top grades, athleticism, and beauty. She is also a Wizard of the AST, determined to avenge her parents by killing Spirits, but later becomes the eighth Spirit to appear in the series.
